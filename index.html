Newport, RI
$325–485 / person

New England's sailing capital — cobblestone waterfront, Gilded Age mansions, world-class seafood

🚗 3.5 hrs drive 🏠 Big Airbnb downtown ⚓ Sailing charter
Why it's great
  • Argia schooner sailing cruise — perfect group activity
  • Thames St & Bowen's Wharf: elite waterfront dining + bars
  • Cliff Walk: 3.5-mile ocean trail past Vanderbilt mansions
  • Polo matches every Saturday in June — free to watch
  • Completely different feel from anywhere else on this list

Annapolis, MD
$300–440 / person

America's sailing capital — peak crab season, Naval Academy, Chesapeake waterfront charm

🚗 2.5 hrs drive 🏠 Big Airbnb downtown 🦀 Peak blue crab
Why it's great
  • June = peak Maryland blue crab season — messy crab feast is the move
  • Woodwind schooner sailing charter right off City Dock
  • Walkable historic district: cobblestones, waterfront, great restaurants
  • Ego Alley: watch mega-yachts cruise through downtown

Newport energy, Chesapeake soul. If you want to eat crabs with wooden mallets on a waterfront deck, this is it.

Artsy river towns, farm-to-table dining, wineries, rolling countryside — Hudson & Rhinebeck

🚗 3–3.5 hrs drive 🏠 Countryside Airbnb 🍷 Wineries + galleries
Why it's great
  • Hudson town: incredible restaurants punching way above their size
  • Rhinebeck: charming, walkable, great food and boutique shopping

Lake Wallenpaupack, PA

5,700-acre Pocono lake — private dock, pontoon rentals, jet skis, fire pit nights

🚗 2–2.5 hrs drive 🏠 Lakefront Airbnb w/ dock ⛵ Full boat rentals
Why it's great
  • Closest and cheapest option — most budget left for activities
  • Pontoon boats, slide boats, Tiki boat, jet skis all rentable

The "lake house" trip. Less about exploring a town, more about waking up on the water every morning and not wanting to leave.
